<h1>The X-Files Episodes</h1>
<h2>Season 1</h2>
<h3>Pilot</h3>
<p>In the <i>Pilot</i> episode, two FBI agents, Fox Mulder and Dana Scully, are partnered up to investigate the X files, which are FBI cases involving unexplained phenominon. In their first case together, they must investigate a series of mysterious deaths in a small Oregon town, where teens are dying--the only connection strange marks on their backs.</p>
<h3>Deep Throat</h3>
<p>Mulder and Scully investigate a case involving a test pilot who disappeared after expressing fears about his job at a military base. Mulder makes contact with a government informent regarding the nature of aliens.</p>
<h3>Squeeze</h3>
<p>The agents investigate a series of murders where the victims were squeezed to death and had their livers removed.</p>
